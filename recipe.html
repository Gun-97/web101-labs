Khmer Food

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Coconut milk
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the fish into small pieces.
  2. Mix coconut milk, kroeung paste, fish sauce, sugar, and egg in a bowl.
  3. Add the fish pieces into the mixture and mix gently.
  4. Place banana leaves in a small bowl or cup.
  5. Pour the fish mixture into the banana leaf cup.
  6. Steam it for about 20 to 30 minutes until the fish is cooked.
  7. Add a little coconut cream and sliced kaffir lime leaves on top.
  8. Serve hot with rice.
Nutrient Amount
Calories 350 kcal
